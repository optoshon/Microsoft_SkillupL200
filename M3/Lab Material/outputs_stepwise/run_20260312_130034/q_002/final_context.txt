4.5 Induction and Training
4.5.1 Mandatory induction shall consist of a minimum of 16 hours of classroom sessions and 8 hours of e-learning within the first 14 days.
4.5.2 A minimum assessment score of 70% is required for certification of completion.

---

6. Learning Records and Assessment

6.1 All training participation, assessment scores, and certifications shall be recorded in the Learning Management System (LMS).
6.2 Certification exams and post-training assessments shall require a minimum passing score of 70% unless otherwise specified by regulatory standards.
6.3 Training effectiveness shall be evaluated annually using Kirkpatrick Level 1–3 metrics and linked to performance outcomes.

5. Linkage to Performance and Career Progression

5.1 Completion of mandatory training is a prerequisite for performance rating normalization under Performance Appraisal Policy (HR-POL-006), Clause 4.4.
5.2 Promotion eligibility requires completion of all role-mandated certifications and leadership readiness programs as specified in HR-POL-006, Clause 5.3.
5.3 Employees with repeated training non-compliance may be placed on corrective development plans or Performance Improvement Plans (PIP).
