3. Definitions

3.1

Protected Characteristics:

Gender, sex, marital status, pregnancy, disability, age, religion, caste, ethnicity, sexual orientation, and any other category protected under applicable law.
3.2

Discrimination:

Any distinction, exclusion, or preference that nullifies or impairs equality of opportunity or treatment in employment.
3.3

Harassment:

Unwelcome conduct, whether verbal, physical, or visual, that creates a hostile or intimidating work environment.
3.4

---

This policy establishes the commitment of ABC Solutions Pvt. Ltd. to provide equal opportunity in all aspects of employment and to prevent discrimination, harassment, and retaliation in compliance with Articles 14, 15, and 16 of the Constitution of India, the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013, and applicable labour laws
